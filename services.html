What We Do

Full-Service Auto Repair

From routine maintenance to complex engine repairs — we handle it all with precision and care. Every job done right the first time.

01

General Service

Comprehensive maintenance to keep your vehicle performing at its best. Oil changes, fluid checks, filter replacements, and multi-point inspections for all makes and models.

02

Tune Ups

Restore engine performance and fuel efficiency. Spark plugs, ignition systems, fuel system cleaning, air filter replacement, and full engine diagnostics.

03

MA Inspection

Licensed Massachusetts state inspection station. Safety and emissions checks done quickly and thoroughly — we're certified to inspect your vehicle.

04

A/C Service

Leak detection, system evacuation, recharge, and component repair. We'll have your A/C blowing cold again — guaranteed comfort all year round.

Check Engine Light

Advanced diagnostic tools to read OBD-II codes and pinpoint the exact problem — saving you time and money on accurate repairs.

06

Exhaust

Mufflers, catalytic converters, exhaust pipes, and gaskets — repair and replacement to keep you running clean and compliant with emissions standards.

Oil Leaks

Valve cover gaskets, oil pans, seals — we find the source and fix it properly to protect your engine from costly damage.

Brakes

Pad replacement, rotor resurfacing, brake fluid flush, and caliper repair. Your safety is our top priority — don't wait on brake service.

09

Tire Repairs

Puncture repairs, rotations, new tire installation, and wheel balancing. Proper tire maintenance extends tire life and improves fuel economy.

Suspensions

Shocks, struts, ball joints, tie rods — we diagnose and repair for a smooth, controlled ride. Say goodbye to bumpy roads.
